Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At TraceBack, we take your privacy seriously. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, post a listing, or contact us. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and any details about items you report.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to facilitate the recovery of lost items, communicate with you about your listings, and improve our services. Your contact information is shared with other users only when you explicitly post a listing.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may use third-party services for hosting and analytics.
